Sigi Gradwohl

Sigi Gradwohl is an Israeli-Swiss-American triple citizen from New York. Training includes the BU Certificate of Fine Arts program and the London Dramatic Academy. She has also studied with Lesly Kahn, John Rosenfeld, and Margie Haber studios. Sigi has guest-starred on Chelsea Handler's After Lately and Disney's Jessie. She has starred in several independent films and webseries, including Stoner Chicks, Hit of the Party, and Game Night. Sigi has performed on Off-Broadway shows in NYC. Credits include A Devil Inside, Bridge to Terabithia, Finding the Sun, Words Words Words, A Few Good Men, and Laughter on the 23rd Floor. Aside from performing and improvising at UCB Comedy, Sigi lives for coffee, bubble gum, and melted soy cheese.

Scenie Award Winner of Outstanding Performance in a Drama
